Hockey Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 27 - August 9, 2024
  • Venue: Stade Yves-du-Manoir, Colombes, Paris
  • Participating Teams:
    • Men's Tournament: 12 teams
    • Women's Tournament: 12 teams

Format

Group Stage: Teams were divided into two pools, with each team playing the others in their pool.

Knockout Stage: The top four teams from each pool advanced to the quarterfinals, leading to semifinals and medal matches.

Men's Hockey Tournament Results

  • Gold Medal: Australia

    Defeated Belgium 3-2 in a thrilling final that went to a shootout after a 1-1 draw in regulation time.

  • Silver Medal: Belgium

    A strong performance throughout the tournament but narrowly lost in the final.

  • Bronze Medal: Netherlands

    Defeated India 4-1 in the bronze medal match.

Women's Hockey Tournament Results

  • Gold Medal: Netherlands

    Dominated the final, defeating Argentina 2-0, showcasing their superior skills and teamwork.

  • Silver Medal: Argentina

    Reached the final with strong performances but couldn’t overcome the Dutch in the end.

  • Bronze Medal: Australia

    Secured the bronze medal after a 3-2 win over Germany in a closely contested match.

Notable Moments

Key Players

  • Blake Govers (Australia, Men’s): Instrumental in Australia’s success with his scoring prowess.
  • Eva de Goede (Netherlands, Women’s): A standout player leading the Dutch team to victory.
  • Agustina Albertario (Argentina, Women’s): Key to Argentina’s silver medal run.
