Badminton Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 27 - August 5, 2024
  • Venue: Porte de la Chapelle Arena, Paris, France

Overview

Badminton at the Paris 2024 Olympics featured intense competition across five events, showcasing the speed, skill, and agility of top players from around the world.

  • Men's Singles
  • Women's Singles
  • Men's Doubles
  • Women's Doubles
  • Mixed Doubles

The tournament format included a group stage followed by knockout rounds, leading to thrilling quarterfinals, semifinals, and medal matches.

Key Events and Results

Men's Singles

  • Gold: Viktor Axelsen (Denmark)
  • Silver: Kento Momota (Japan)
  • Bronze: Anthony Sinisuka Ginting (Indonesia)

Women's Singles

  • Gold: Chen Yufei (China)
  • Silver: Tai Tzu-Ying (Chinese Taipei)
  • Bronze: Akane Yamaguchi (Japan)

Men's Doubles

  • Gold: Marcus Fernaldi Gideon / Kevin Sanjaya Sukamuljo (Indonesia)
  • Silver: Takeshi Kamura / Keigo Sonoda (Japan)
  • Bronze: Mohammad Ahsan / Hendra Setiawan (Indonesia)

Women's Doubles

  • Gold: Chen Qingchen / Jia Yifan (China)
  • Silver: Yuki Fukushima / Sayaka Hirota (Japan)
  • Bronze: Lee So-hee / Shin Seung-chan (South Korea)

Mixed Doubles

  • Gold: Zheng Siwei / Huang Yaqiong (China)
  • Silver: Dechapol Puavaranukroh / Sapsiree Taerattanachai (Thailand)
  • Bronze: Yuta Watanabe / Arisa Higashino (Japan)
