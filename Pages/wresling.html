Wrestling Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 28 - August 10, 2024
  • Venue: Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, Paris

Overview

Wrestling at the Paris 2024 Olympics will feature both Freestyle and Greco-Roman disciplines. Wrestlers compete in various weight categories and aim to pin their opponent's shoulders to the mat or score points through various techniques.
