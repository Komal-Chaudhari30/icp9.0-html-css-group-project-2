Athletics at the Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Dates: August 2 - August 11, 2024

Venue: Stade de France, Paris

Overview

Athletics is one of the most prominent and widely followed events in the Olympic Games, featuring a wide array of track and field events, including sprints, middle-distance, long-distance races, hurdles, relays, jumps, throws, and combined events (decathlon and heptathlon).
