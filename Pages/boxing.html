Boxing Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 28 - August 10, 2024
  • Venue: Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, Paris

Overview

Boxing at the Paris 2024 Olympics will feature men’s and women’s events across various weight categories. The competition will follow the standard Olympic boxing format, where athletes compete in a series of rounds to score points or achieve a knockout.
