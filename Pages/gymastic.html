Gymnastics Overview at Paris 2024 Olympics

Event Details

  • Event Dates: July 28 - August 10, 2024
  • Venue: Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, Paris

Overview

The gymnastics competition at the Paris 2024 Olympics will feature both artistic and rhythmic gymnastics, as well as trampoline. Athletes will compete in various events, showcasing their strength, flexibility, and precision.
